Ordering and checkout

One line order form

This compact form is used whenever an object (e.g. Publication, Topological Map) should be added to the shopping cart.

Compact form on one line 
<form class="form-inline" role="form">

	<fieldset>
		<legend>Compact form on one line</legend>

		<!-- Number input -->
		<div class="form-group">
			<label for="inputNumber" class="control-label">Number</label>
			<input type="number" class="form-control" size="2" id="inputNumber" placeholder="5">
		</div>

		<!-- Dropdown -->
		<div class="form-group">
			<label class="control-label hidden" for="dropdown">Dropdown</label>
			<select id="dropdown" class="form-control">
        <option>Choose your language ...</option>
        <option>Option 1</option>
        <option>Option 2</option>
      </select>
		</div>

		<button type="submit" class="btn btn-primary">Add to basket</button>

	</fieldset>
</form>

Shopping cart

By default, the shopping cart is not shown. Only after an object has been added to the shopping cart does the latter appear in the context pane. It then remains visible until it is either completely deleted or the purchase is finalized.
